Art and Culture Center/Hollywood

A beautiful Textpattern CMS powered website, featuring a magazine-style layout, created for the Art and Culture Center/Hollywood. The homepage dynamically pulls content from current and future exhibitions and upcoming events, as well as manually selected feature articles.
