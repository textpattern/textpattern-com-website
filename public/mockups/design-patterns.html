Design patterns

Article containing examples of various inline and block elements that should be considered and styled within the brand websites. Supported browsers: Apple Safari 5+, Google Chrome 30+, Microsoft Internet Explorer 8+, Mozilla Firefox 24+, Opera Browser 12+. Development environment requires Sass and Compass, plus the Compass extensions Breakpoint and Singularity.
